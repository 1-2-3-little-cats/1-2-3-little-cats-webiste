What is our objective?

1, 2, 3 little cats aims at being the #1 qualitative website for act adoption and care. Not only we want to provide you with relevant information and help about your little furry ball, but we also want to share our passion for cats with you!

To reach this objective, we have developed this website and we are very proud to present it to you today! The website will often be updated and enriched with more and more content. So stay tuned!

Our legitimacy

Both of us have been taking care of cats for many years and have a considerable experience in cat breeding, well-being and food. Before getting this website online, we went through dozens of books by the most famous and recognized cat behaviorists all over the world and compiled all this data in a fun and accessible way.

In addition to that, our articles are built in collaboration with veterinaries and are systematically verified by a committee of experts to make sure we provide our readers with 100% accurrate information.
